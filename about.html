Our Story

"Bean & Beyond" started from the vision of creating a special space for coffee lovers with extraordinary taste and inspiring experiences. Founded in 2023 in the city center, Bean & Beyond is dedicated to serve the best global coffee.

The Story Behind Our Logo

Bean: Refers to coffee beans, highlighting the main focus on coffee as our main product.

Beyond: Emphasizes that the website offers more than just coffee. This creates the impression that on this website, visitors can enjoy various aspects of the world of coffee from taste to variations of other coffee brands.

That is why we partnered with 3 big name coffee companies, Starbucks, Maxx Coffee and Lain Hati Coffee to offer our customers the best coffee possible.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: Elevate coffee experiences by sourcing the finest beans and creating a community that celebrates the joy of exceptional coffee.

Mission: Craft and deliver unparalleled coffee moments, fostering a culture of sustainability, quality, and connection in every cup.
